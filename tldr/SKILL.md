---
name: tldr
description: Summarize URLs, files, or pasted text in 5 sentences with a bold takeaway. Use for "summarize", "TL;DR", "too long", or "what is this?" requests.
---
Read the source. Return exactly 5 sentences: first answer what it is; the rest cover what matters. Bold the takeaway sentence. Append exactly `Do I care? — <yes/no/maybe> — <one reason>.` Nothing else: no headers, lists, analysis, or follow-up questions.
